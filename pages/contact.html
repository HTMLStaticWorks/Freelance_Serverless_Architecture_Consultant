Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our serverless consulting process.

How fast can you start an audit?

Once the intake form is submitted, we typically schedule a kickoff session within 48 business hours to review your architecture blueprints.

Do you work with hybrid clouds?

Absolutely. We specialize in multi-region and multi-cloud serverless networking, including complex setups like AWS Lambda + Cloudflare Workers.

Is there an SLA guarantee?

Our Enterprise and Retainer packages include strict SLA guarantees for system latency remediations and 24/7 technical support response.

What level of access do you require?

We use Read-Only cross-account IAM roles or temporary session credentials to audit your FaaS configurations and CloudWatch telemetry logs safely.

Do you offer implementation services?

Yes, besides audits, we provide hands-on implementation for AWS SAM, Serverless Framework, and Terraform infrastructure-as-code updates.

What is the typical ROI on an audit?

Most clients see a 30-50% reduction in cloud billing within the first 30 days of implementation by optimizing memory allocations and DB connection pooling.

Do you support Pulumi or CDK?

Yes, while our default templates are SAM/Terraform, we have extensive experience with Pulumi (TypeScript/Go) and the AWS Cloud Development Kit (CDK).
